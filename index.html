Welcome to Game Haven

The Game Haven is a local video game store that aims to provide customers with the best selection of video games in Portland, Oregon. The Game Haven has an annual operational cost of $50,000 and makes roughly $40,000 a year in gross revenue. Game Haven sells roughly 1,000 games a year to its customers. On average, Game Haven sells to about 500 customers a year. The shop sells games from 4 different platforms: PC, Xbox, Playstation, and Switch. Game Haven sells games from over 30 different genres. The shop is trying to expand into e-commerce so a database system is implemented to organize the inventory and handle the relationships between customers and orders. This system will help track inventory and analyze any thefts made in-store or underperforming video games. The system will also help optimize sales by tracking which genres and game platforms are popular.
